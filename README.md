Sales-Performance-Dashboard
This Dashboard represent How many sales have been made by Sales Executive in various state. Here is a breakdown of how the data is visualized:

1> Regional Sales Overview (Slicers).
    At the top, I have integrated City-based Slicers (Chennai, Delhi, Mumbai, etc.). 
    How it works: Selecting a city instantly filters all charts. 
    Insight: This allows management to compare performance between different states and cities like Surat vs Nagpur effortlessly.

2> Individual Sales Performance (Top 5 Bar Chart).
    The bar chart titled "Top 5 Total Sales by Executive" identifies the highest contributors. 
    Analysis: In the current view, Jagdish Chandra is leading with 389 units, closely followed by Rachita Anupam (385 units) and Miny Mole (384 units). 
    Business Use: This helps in identifying top-performing employees for rewards or recognition.

3> Efficiency Tracking (Target Hit Ratio).
    The Pie Chart represents the Target Hit Ratio. 
    Analysis: It breaks down the percentage of the target achieved by the team. We can see segments like 23%, 22%, and 19%, indicating a balanced distribution of sales achievements across the selected group. 
    Goal: The closer these numbers are to the total target, the more efficient the team is.

4> Gap Analysis (Away from Target % Line Graph).
    The line graph titled "Away from Target in %" is crucial for identifying underperformance. 
    Analysis: It shows how far each executive is from their 100% goal. For instance, Omprakash O is currently 71.40% away from his target, which is a high gap compared to others like Prabha Desikan (65.80%). 
    Business Action: This visual highlights which salesmen need more training or support to bridge the gap.
